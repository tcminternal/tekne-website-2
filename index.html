OUR THOUGHTS

At Tekne, our research process is exhaustive. We believe that true understanding comes from boots-on-the-ground due diligence, not just spreadsheet modeling. Our team frequently travels to key technology hubs—from Shenzhen to Bangalore, from Taipei to Sao Paulo—meeting with suppliers, competitors, and customers to form a holistic view of the competitive landscape.

This approach allows us to identify dislocations in market pricing before they become consensus. By focusing on the difference between a company's current market value and its long-term utility value, we construct a portfolio designed to compound capital efficiently over time, regardless of short-term market noise or macroeconomic headwinds.
